  3. Jovem Nerd (young nerd)

    This is my favorite podcast, the Nerdcast is starred by Jovem Nerd (Alexandre Otonni) and Azaghal(Deive Pazos)

    In this show they talk about everything in a funny, sarcastic and proficient way.

    They talk about pop and geek culture, and have podcasts extras about technology called nerdTech, English learning (Speak English), entrepeneurship (empreendedor) and NerdCash about manage your finances.
